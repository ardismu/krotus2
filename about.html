Who we are?

KROTUS GROUP LTD is a design and build construction company structured around the key disciplines of design, engineering, interior fit out and project management.
We seek to provide affordable and innovative construction solutions helping clients transform their great designs to reality.
Our group is geared towards investment in public, social and commercial infrastructure.

Our Mission, Vision & Strategy

Our vision is to be the most sought after and sustainable business in our industry.
Our purpose is to build the foundations of a sustainable future by enhancing the communities in which we all live, learn, play and protect.

Where we operate?

KROTUS GROUP LTD can take up on any projects both locally and internationally.
We have operate on East African boundaries, with hopes of expanding to the rest of the world soon.
