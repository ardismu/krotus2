About Us

Our Mission & Vision

KROTUS GROUP LTD is a design and build construction company structured around the key disciplines of design, engineering, interior fit out and project management.
We seek to provide affordable and innovative construction solutions helping clients transform their great designs to reality.
Our group is geared towards investment in public, social and commercial infrastructure.

Our Strategy

We are KROTUS GROUP Ltd, our vision is to be the most sought after and sustainable business in our industry. Our purpose is to build the foundations of a sustainable future by enhancing the communities in which we all live, learn, play and protect.

Where we operate

We have proudly been handling projects in Kenya and Tanzania (with a view of expansion into the East Africa region).

Construction Services

We carry out major infrastructure projects, engineering and design, new build construction, interior fit out and refurbishment for the wider public and commercial sectors.
