Our Core Values

Our values are what we have judged are most important to how we want to work:

  • Professionalism

    We relentlessly aim to demonstrate expertise, efficiency, and competence in execution of projects to the highest standards.

  • Teamwork

    Great teamwork allows Krotus Group Ltd to benefit from the combined strength of our individual talents, skills and expertise which are vital when tackling challenging construction projects.

  • Integrity

    We act in the highest standards of moral and ethical values. We deal fairly with our business partners

  • Health & Safety

    • We integrate safety and risk management into our daily operations ensuring the construction site is safe as reasonably possible for those who work on it.

  • Environmental

    We take every caution to impact positively on our surroundings through sustainable construction.

  • Innovation

    We strive to stay on top of advancing technology, growth within our industry, and changing needs of our clients. Through our passion for construction and forward-thinking project management style, we continuously diversify our skill set to set us apart.

We will continue to build on our culture where everyone at Krotus has equal access to opportunities and our people advance on merit.

Our competitive advantage stems from the collective experiences, perspectives and backgrounds of our workforce. Leveraging this diversity provides innovative solutions for an increasingly challenging environment.

We seek to create rewarding career opportunities for our employee-owners and strengthen and support our industry and communities.
