Welcome to Krotus

KROTUS GROUP LTD is a privately owned construction company which teams up with a group of creative professional consultants to service our clients with our collective experience. It is a multidisciplinary construction company structured around the key disciplines of architecture, interior design, landscape architecture, structural design, building and project management, with integrated sustainability and assured final product.

What we do

KROTUS GROUP Ltd takes pride in its ability to creatively deliver construction and deliver outcomes that are driven by understanding clients' needs. The results are unique and exceed client expectations and as we put it, we are “Committed to Building Excellence".

Why choose us

We seek to provide affordable, smart and innovative construction that reflects the advancement of technology. We are committed to developing modern state of the art construction, successfully and within the set project time lines and a stress-free relation with our clients.
